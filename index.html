Process

From cash to digital in seconds.

1
Get coins from an agent
Hand cash to a Zillion agent at your local market or shop. They issue you Ed25519-signed coins — stored encrypted on your phone.
2
Send value to anyone
Share your .zil file via WhatsApp, show a QR code, or tap phones with NFC. No internet. Instant. The recipient gets verified coins.
3
Pay at any merchant
Merchants scan your QR or receive your file. They see the verified amount instantly. No waiting, no network, no fees.
4
Cash out whenever you want
Return to any Zillion agent to redeem your coins for physical naira. Your coins, your terms, your timing.

Security

Bank-grade protection.
No bank required.

🔏
Ed25519 Signed
Every coin is signed by ZILLION-MINT-01 using elliptic curve cryptography. Impossible to forge.
📱
AES-256 on Device
Your vault is encrypted locally. Even if your phone is lost, your coins stay locked.
🛡️
3-Layer Fraud Prevention
Local dedup + server validation + Supabase registry. No coin can be spent twice.
🔗
Full Traceability
Every coin carries a cryptographic chain from mint to redemption. Admin can audit any transaction.
